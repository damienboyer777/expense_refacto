Three findings we anchored the design on

Mobile submission is a strength, not the problem. The pain starts after you hit submit.

01
Status opacity. Employees submit an expense and enter a black box. No visibility on approval, no signal when refused, no clear path to resubmit.
02
Mileage trust gap. The IK estimate shown in-app doesn't match the payslip amount (French tax brackets). Every surprise erodes trust.
03
One-by-one submission fatigue. Field workers and frequent submitters pile up receipts because submitting 8 expenses means repeating the same form 8 times.

Chapter03

Three explorations, three pain points.

On top of the migration, we've been exploring three parallel tracks — each tied to one of the research findings. These are where we want your reality check on faisabilité, effort, and risk.

II.

Mileage · estimation transparency

Close the gap between what we show and what they get.

Short-term fix for the IK bracket mismatch: surface the fact that the displayed amount is an estimation, not the final payslip value. Uses the Unity Informational Alert under the Montant field, with tooltip on the list view. No logic change — purely a trust & clarity layer. A deeper module redesign is scoped for Q2 2026.

III.

Bulk submission · the batch tray

8 receipts, one flow — not eight.

Interactive prototype (Model A): batch tray with accordion cards, drag-and-drop, simulated OCR outcomes, failed-OCR states, and manual entry as hybrid card. Client-side batching submits as individual expenses, so the backend contract doesn't change. OCR POC running in parallel (Mindee v5 / AWS / HF) — this is where we need you most.

Status, at a glance, before the list.

Chapter04

What we want to talk about.

Rather than a flat Q&A, here's the shape of the conversation I'd like us to have — feel free to push back on any of it.

Topic 01 · Tech debt

Dual codebase & the new repo strategy

  • Where does Unity migration land — Jetlang admin or Classic employee?
  • Are we anchoring migration to the new repo / event-driven architecture work?
  • What's the minimum fallback path if we ship incrementally?
Topic 02 · OCR & bulk

OCR POC, provider, and contract

  • State of the Mindee v5 / AWS / HF benchmark — any early signals?
  • How do failure modes from OCR map onto the batch tray states?
  • Client-side batching vs. true bulk endpoint — what's the cost delta?
Topic 03 · Data freshness

Status signals in near-real-time

  • Overview cards + stepper + toasts all rely on fresh status data.
  • Polling, push, or event-driven — what's realistic for S1?
  • What does "approved / refused" latency look like today?
Topic 04 · Scope & sequencing

What lands in S1, what slips to S2

  • Migration is the floor — non-negotiable.
  • Rejection loop + status stepper: highest user impact, lowest tech risk.
  • Bulk: high impact, highest tech risk — can we ring-fence?
  • IK deep redesign → Q2 2026, but the estimation fix should ship S1.
